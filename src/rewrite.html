Safety model

What this extension does and does not do

Does

  • Generate ready prompts and rewrite requests locally.
  • Copy prompts to clipboard after your click.
  • Open ChatGPT in a normal tab.
  • Store history locally if enabled.

Does not

  • Use OpenAI API.
  • Read ChatGPT cookies.
  • Submit messages automatically.
  • Scrape ChatGPT responses.
  • Send your text to a backend.
